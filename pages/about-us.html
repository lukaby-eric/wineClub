About Us

Louie’s Wine Dive is proud to offer you membership to our Wine Club. Club members will receive carefully selected and exceptional handcrafted wine every month, with introductions and tasting notes from the Louie’s Wine Dive team of certified sommeliers. The team has planned a guided journey for Club members encompassing wine selections from the most celebrated winemakers and wine regions on Earth, as well as the new and lesser known vineyards offering tremendous quality and value.
Join a community of wine lovers centered around your local Louie's Wine Dive and enjoy monthly community events like the Wine Club monthly tasting, members-only access to small lots of wine, and early access to winemaker dinners, tastings and other VIP experiences.
Louie's Wine Dive was founded in 2012 as a place for food and wine lovers to be social. Food and beverage is the cornerstone of our experience. It is magical to us. We've built our lives around it. And we are thrilled to be launching the next phase with Louie's Wine Club.

Why Louie's Wine Club?

Learn more with tasting notes from our team of sommeliers.

But don't just take our word for it! Take notes and follow along with our tasting guide to start picking out details of the wines you love. Or come in to chat with our sommeliers at any Louie's Wine Dive restaurant.

Don't spend money on shipping. Spend money on wine!

Pick up at your convenience at the Louie's Wine Dive location of your choice. No shipping means we're putting more of your dollars toward what matters: sourcing and purchasing top quality wine.

Broaden your perspective with wines from around the world.

Unlike most wine clubs, our team is not pigeonholed in having to use wine of a select brand or region, but rather is procuring wine focused on your enjoyment and building knowledge.
