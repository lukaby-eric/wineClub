Welcome to Louie's Wine Club

We're Glad You're Here

Louie’s Wine Dive is proud to offer you membership to our Wine Club. Club members will receive carefully selected and exceptional handcrafted wine every month, with introductions and tasting notes from the Louie’s Wine Dive team of certified sommeliers. Join a community of wine lovers centered around your local Louie's Wine Dive and enjoy monthly community events like Wine Club Tastings, members-only access to small lots of wine, and early access to winemaker dinners, tastings and other VIP experiences.

MAY 2023

Albarino, Raimat

Varietal: Cabernet Sauvignon

Region: Spain

Wine Club Level: Gold/Plat


Today, Raimat is one of the leading benchmarks in sustainable and organic wine-growing, the result of which is a wide range of wines and cavas of the highest quality. The current landscape of the Raimat estate has little in common with its origins when Manuel Raventós started his dream after acquiring 3,200 hectares of barren land in Lleida. At that time, the estate was just a desolate desert which it was hard to imagine capable of ever bearing fruit. First sight you'll see a pale yellow colour with greenish highlights. Mineral aromas at the start, joined by white flowers, mango, melon and grapefruit. First on your palater is a fresh, citrus sensation, then a long and refreshing

finish dominated by white flowers and melon.

Delicious, handpicked wines.

Direct from our restaurants to you.

For years, Louie's Wine Dive has been bringing wonderful wines from famed winemakers and unknown vineyards to guests in our restaurants. Now, we are pleased to bring you Louie's Wine Club. The team has planned a guided journey for Club members encompassing wine selections from the most celebrated winemakers and wine regions on Earth, as well as the new and lesser known vineyards offering tremendous quality and value. We hope you will join us on this journey.

Why Louie's Wine Club?

Learn more with tasting notes from our team of sommeliers.

But don't just take our word for it! Take notes and follow along with our tasting guide to start picking out details of the wines you love. Or come in to chat with our sommeliers at any Louie's Wine Dive restaurant.

Don't spend money on shipping. Spend money on wine!

Pick up at your convenience at the Louie's Wine Dive location of your choice. No shipping means we're putting more of your dollars toward what matters: sourcing and purchasing top quality wine.

Broaden your perspective with wines from around the world.

Unlike most wine clubs, our team is not pigeonholed in having to use wine of a select brand or region, but rather is procuring wine focused on your enjoyment and building knowledge.
